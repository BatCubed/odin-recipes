Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna recipe is sure to please everyone in your family. Robust tomato sauce, savory cheeses, and tender pasta bring together a classic dish that will make a tasty dinner that will delight the entire table, from the in-laws to the picky eaters! Best of all, this recipe is versatile; any meat (or a vegetarian version with your favorite veggie blend or meat substitute!) will suit the flavors, and won't impact the cook time, as it's prepared separately and added into the sauce before baking.
Give this lovely Italian dish a try, and you certainly won't regret it. With just a little bit of preparation beforehand, you can even freeze it and bake it later on in the week. Hello meal prep, goodbye meal-time panic!
